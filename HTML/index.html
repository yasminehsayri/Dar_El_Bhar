Services

Kitchen :

Dar EL Bhar offres its guests a true culinary experience in its restaurant and bar.

Wellness & Fitness :

The intimate Spa in the Dar El Bhar hotel provides guests with a truly Zen atmosphere that will transport you to a state of total peace and relaxation. The Nuxe Spa features 8 treatment and massage rooms, an indoor swimming pool, a fitness room, treatments with essential oils and facials using only natural ingredients, beauty treatments, a Hammam, and a relaxation area.

Meetings and Events :

The hotel features a Business Centre with rooms that can be used separately or can be combined in a variety of different ways. The Conference Centre has 15 fully equipped meeting rooms that can be accessed separately, along with a 1,500 m² terrace with views overlooking the lake and capacity for 1,000 people.
